Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2024

At PlaySphere Kinetic, we believe your city is your playground. To help you connect with venues, events, and your community safely, we need to collect and use some of your data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better, more personalized experience when you use the PlaySphere platform.

  • check_circle Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • check_circle Location Data: Precise or approximate location to show nearby venues and events (only with your permission).
  • check_circle Activity Data: Games joined, venues visited, and community interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is primarily used to facilitate the core functions of PlaySphere:

  • arrow_right Connecting you with local sports venues and organizing events.
  • arrow_right Improving our services and developing new community features.
  • arrow_right Communicating updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share limited information with venue partners solely for the purpose of fulfilling bookings. We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings. If you wish to permanently close your account and remove your data, please contact our support team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@playsphere.kinetic.
