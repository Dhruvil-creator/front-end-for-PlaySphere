# Project PRD: PlaySphere Ahmedabad – The Urban Playground Ecosystem

## 1. Project Overview
**PlaySphere Ahmedabad** is a premium, AI-native sports ecosystem designed to connect athletes, venue owners, and corporate teams within a unified digital-physical playground. The platform bridges the gap between casual pickup games and professional-grade tournament play, anchored in a high-fidelity "Urban Playground" design language.

## 2. Brand Identity & Vision
*   **Brand Persona**: Kinetic, Premium, Inclusive, and Bold.
*   **Design System**: *PlaySphere Kinetic* – utilizing a vibrant primary orange (#ff5a36), soft clay surfaces, and fluid, bento-style layouts.
*   **Tagline**: Your City, Your Playground.
*   **Target Audience**: 
    *   **Athletes**: Urban dwellers looking for competition, community, and training.
    *   **Venue Partners**: Facility owners requiring professional booking and growth tools.
    *   **Corporate Partners**: Companies building employee engagement through sports leagues.
    *   **Coaches**: Professional trainers managing rosters and performance labs.

## 3. Core Functional Modules

### A. Discovery & Onboarding
*   **Goal**: Frictionless entry into the ecosystem.
*   **Key Features**: Map-based venue discovery, skill-level self-assessment, and "Sports Passport" identity creation.
*   **Flows**: Multistep onboarding (Sport selection -> Area selection -> Skill level -> Success).

### B. The Social Core (Matchmaking)
*   **Goal**: Building the "Digital Locker Room."
*   **Key Features**: Community feed, Match Requests, Squad Recruitment, and real-time "Pulse" stories.
*   **Interactions**: Tactile match-joining, proximity-based athlete discovery, and squad-level chat.

### C. Transactional Engine (Booking & Shop)
*   **Goal**: High-conversion utility.
*   **Key Features**: End-to-end Venue/Court booking, Pro-Coach marketplace, and a premium "Gear Swap" community marketplace.
*   **Financials**: Integrated wallet, secure UPI/Card payments, and transparent refund management.

### D. Competitive Peak (Tournaments)
*   **Goal**: Elevating the game.
*   **Key Features**: Live match commentary, real-time scoreboards, tournament brackets, and city-wide MMR leaderboards.
*   **Elite Layer**: Live performance dashboards tracking heart rate, speed, and intensity for pro-level athletes.

### E. Business & Administrative Portals
*   **Venue Pro**: Operational command for facility owners (calendar management, revenue analytics).
*   **Trainer Pro**: Roster management and performance reporting for coaches.
*   **Corporate Portal**: Employee approval dashboards and team registration for business leagues.
*   **Admin Hub**: Integrity management, verification systems, and system-wide moderation.

## 4. Technical Architecture Highlights
*   **Design Rationale**: Bento-box layouts for data-heavy views; high-contrast "Midnight Arena" mode for competitive modules.
*   **Interconnectivity**: Every screen is part of a non-linear journey (e.g., a "Match Request" notification leads directly to the "Live Arena").
*   **Platform Scale**: 100+ unique screens covering B2C, B2B, and G2C (City-wide analytics) interactions.

## 5. Success Metrics
*   **Match Density**: Number of games successfully organized via the "Find Players" flow.
*   **Partner Growth**: Revenue and utilization increase for Venue Partners.
*   **Community Retention**: Engagement levels in the "Community Feed" and "Squad Chat" modules.
*   **Conversion**: Seamless transition from onboarding to the first paid booking.
