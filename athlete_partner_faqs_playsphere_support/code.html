What is PlaySphere?

expand_more

PlaySphere is your city's ultimate playground network. We connect active individuals with local sports venues, organized pick-up games, and a vibrant community of athletes of all skill levels.

Is PlaySphere free to join?

expand_more

Yes! Creating an account and joining the community is completely free. You only pay when you book a paid venue or join a premium event organized by partners.

How do I join a pick-up game?

expand_more

Navigate to the 'Events' section, filter by your sport of choice, and click 'Join' on any open game. Ensure your skill level matches the game's intent for the best experience!

How can I list my venue?

expand_more

Head over to our 'Partner Program' page (linked in the footer) to apply. Our team will review your facilities and help you set up your digital storefront on PlaySphere.

Still need help?

Our support team is ready to assist you get back in the game.
