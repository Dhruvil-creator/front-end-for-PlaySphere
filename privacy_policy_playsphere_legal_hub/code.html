Policy at a Glance

Quick summary of our key privacy practices. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your data.

Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, request on-demand services, contact customer support, or otherwise communicate with us.

This may include your name, email address, phone number, postal address, profile picture, payment method, items requested, delivery notes, and other information you choose to provide. We are committed to minimizing data collection to only what is necessary for functional platform operations.

How We Use Your Info

PlaySphere utilizes your collected data to facilitate a safe, efficient, and customized experience. Primarily, we use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services, including matchmaking and community features.
  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and receipts.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services to ensure optimal performance.
  • Personalize your experience by presenting content, features, or advertisements tailored to your interests and athlete profile.

Data Security Commitment

We employ industry-standard encryption protocols and robust physical security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your trust is our highest priority, and we routinely audit our systems to maintain compliance with international data protection regulations.

Athlete Rights

As a member of the PlaySphere community, you retain comprehensive control over your personal data. We empower you with the following rights regarding your information:

Right to Access

You may request a copy of all personal data we currently hold about you. This information will be provided in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your personal data from our systems, subject to certain legal and operational exceptions (e.g., fraud prevention or ongoing disputes).

Right to Rectification

If you believe your data is inaccurate or incomplete, you can update your profile directly within the app or contact our support team to amend your records immediately.
